Kenny and Sarah

Hello! We'll be getting married on January 2, 2014 at Walt Disney World in Florida! This page is your one-stop shop for information about our special day.

RSVP

Just give us a heads up by email or give us a call. With such a small group, we're not worrying about formal RSVPs. Just let us know whether you're coming and whether you're bringing a guest, and that should be plenty.

Travel

You'll want to fly into Orlando International Airport (MCO). You shouldn't need a rental car—Disney's Magical Express can take you from the airport to your resort.

Lodging

We've blocked rooms at two Disney resorts, beginning the night of Monday, December 30 and ending the night of Saturday, January 4:

  • Port Orleans Riverside - $138/night
  • All-Star Movies - $77/night

You can book rooms at these resorts by calling (407)827-6799. You can purchase discounted theme park tickets through the same number.

Gifts

Don't you dare. Your gift to us is your presence at our wedding.
